Doom Metal

Doom metal is an extreme subgenre of heavy metal music that typically uses slower tempos, low-tuned guitars and a much "thicker" or "heavier" sound than other heavy metal genres. Both the music and the lyrics intend to evoke a sense of despair, dread, and impending doom.

Candlemass

Candlemass is a Swedish doom metal band from Stockholm, formed in 1984.

Electric Wizard

Electric Wizard are an English doom metal band from Dorset, formed in 1993.

Reverend Bizarre

Reverend Bizarre was a doom metal band from Finland, formed in 1995.

Melodic Death metal

Melodic death metal is a subgenre of death metal that employs highly melodic guitar riffs, often borrowing from traditional heavy metal. The style originated and developed in Sweden and the United Kingdom around 1993.

At the Gates

At the Gates is a Swedish death metal band from Gothenburg, formed in 1990.

Amon Amarth

Amon Amarth is a Swedish melodic death metal band from Tumba, formed in 1992.

Arch Enemy

Arch Enemy is a Swedish melodic death metal band, formed in 1995.

Black Metal

Black metal is an extreme subgenre of heavy metal music. Common traits include fast tempos, a shrieking vocal style, heavily distorted guitars played with tremolo picking, raw (lo-fi) recording, unconventional song structures, and an emphasis on atmosphere. Artists often appear in corpse paint and adopt pseudonyms.

Burzum

Burzum is a Norwegian music project founded by Varg Vikernes in 1991.

Mayhem

Mayhem is a Norwegian black metal band formed in 1984 in Oslo, Norway.

Carpathian Forest

Carpathian Forest is a Norwegian black metal band formed in 1992.
